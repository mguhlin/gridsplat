# Credits and Sources

## TEKS

TEKS activity alignment is structured from Texas Education Agency TEKS source pages:

- Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills overview: https://tea.texas.gov/curriculum/teks/
- Mathematics TEKS page: https://tea.texas.gov/node/102458
- Science TEKS page: https://tea.texas.gov/educators/subject-areas/science/science

The activity data marks standards with `needsHumanReview: true` until a Texas educator verifies current official codes and wording before publication.
